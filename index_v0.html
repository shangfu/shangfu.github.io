Biography

I am a doctoral student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. I work with Prof. Hanan Samet in the Institute for Advanced Computer Studies (UMIACS). My research interests include Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Distributed System and Machine Learning.
